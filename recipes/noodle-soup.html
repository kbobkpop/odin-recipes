Very Good Noodle Soup Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Two packages of instant noodles (Yum are very good)
  • Two soft boiled eggs
  • A handful of frozen soy beans
  • Three broccoli florets

Instructions:

  1. Set one liter of water to boil
  2. Empty the noodle packages and the spices within into a bowl of appropriate size
  3. Peel the two eggs and put them in the same bowl
  4. Put the beans and broccoli in a separate bowl
  5. Fill the two bowls with boiled water
  6. Wait a couple of minutes
  7. Pour out the water from the broccoli and the beans
  8. Put the broccoli and the beans into the soup
  9. Enjoy!
